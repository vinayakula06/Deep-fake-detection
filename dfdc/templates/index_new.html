How It Works

🎞️

Frame Sampling

15 frames are uniformly sampled across the video using np.linspace for consistent temporal coverage.

👤

MTCNN Face Detection

MTCNN detects and crops faces with 30% margin padding. Resolution-aware scaling ensures accuracy across all video sizes.

🧠

EfficientNet-B7 NS

7 independently trained EfficientNet-B7 Noisy Student models form a powerful ensemble for robust prediction.

📊

Confident Strategy

Heuristic aggregation detects videos with >11 high-confidence fake frames and weights predictions accordingly.

⚡

GPU Accelerated

CUDA autocast (FP16) dramatically speeds up inference. Falls back gracefully to CPU when no GPU is available.

🖼️

Image Support

Supports both videos and static images. Single images are processed through the full face detection and inference pipeline.
